FAQ
WHAT EXACTLY IS SUET?
Suet is the fat that surrounds a cows kidneys. Now, I know it sounds weird to put beef fat on your skin, but it has great benefits!
WHAT ARE THE BENEFITS?
One of the biggest advantages of using tallow is that it's biocompatible with our skin. That means its chemical makeup is very similar to the makeup of our skin.
WHY IS THIS IMPORTANT?
The reason this is important is it allows the tallow to blend into the skin, absorbing quickly to provide moisture and nutrients.
DOES IT SMELL LIKE FAT?
Tallow loses the majority - if not all - of its fatty smell before reaching your skin. This is thanks to the rendering process that makes the scent undetectable.
DOES IT FEEL GREASY?
Due to the rendering process and its molecular makeup, tallow has a very non-greasy, moisturizing, and smooth texture.
IS IT BETTER FOR MY SKIN?
Tallow adds moisture to the skin without making you greasy. It works great for dry, combination, and oily skin types. Even those with sensitive skin!
WHAT ARE THE INGREDIENTS?
Pure tallow has only one ingredient: TALLOW! This keeps the ingredients minimal and preservative free. Making for a simple and safe product to add to your routine.
